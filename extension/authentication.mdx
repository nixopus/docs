---
title: "Authentication"
description: "Sign in to Nixopus from your editor."
---

The extension uses a device authorization flow to connect your Nixopus account. You approve the connection in your browser — no passwords or tokens pasted into the editor.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Nixopus sidebar">
    Click the Nixopus icon in the activity bar. If you're not signed in, you'll see a **Sign In with Browser** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize in your browser">
    Click the button. Your browser opens to the Nixopus authorization page. A device code is displayed in the sidebar — enter it in your browser to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Once approved, the extension is authenticated. You can close the browser tab and return to your editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Self-hosted users authenticate with email and password by default.
</Note>

<Note>
  Sessions persist across editor restarts. You only need to sign in again if you explicitly sign out or your session expires.
</Note>
