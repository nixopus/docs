---
title: "Installation"
description: "Install the Nixopus extension for VS Code or Cursor."
---

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="VS Code">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Extensions panel">
        Press `Ctrl+Shift+X` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+X` (macOS) to open Extensions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search and install">
        Search for **Nixopus** and click **Install**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in">
        Open the Nixopus sidebar and follow the [authentication flow](/extension/authentication) to connect your account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Cursor">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Extensions panel">
        Press `Cmd+Shift+X` (macOS) or `Ctrl+Shift+X` (Windows/Linux) to open Extensions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search and install">
        Search for **Nixopus** and click **Install**. The extension is fully compatible with Cursor.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in">
        Open the Nixopus sidebar and follow the [authentication flow](/extension/authentication) to connect your account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  If you're running a self-hosted instance, update the extension settings to point to your own domain before signing in. See [Configuration](/extension/configuration).
</Tip>
