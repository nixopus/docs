---
title: "Notifications"
description: "Get alerted about deployments and events."
---

Nixopus can notify you over email, Slack, or Discord when deployments succeed, fail, or need attention.

## Open notification settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar footer to open the settings modal. Select **Notifications** from the categories.

## Channels

The **Channels** tab lets you configure where notifications are sent:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email (SMTP)">
    Configure your SMTP provider to send email notifications. Fill in host, port, username, password, from address, and encryption settings.

    <Note>
      For Gmail, use an [App Password](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/185833) instead of your account password.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Slack">
    Connect a Slack workspace to receive deployment notifications in a channel.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Discord">
    Connect a Discord server to receive deployment notifications via webhook.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Preferences

Switch to the **Preferences** tab to control which notifications you receive. Preferences are grouped into:

- **Activity notifications** — Deploy started, deploy succeeded, deploy failed, and other deployment events.
- **Security notifications** — Login alerts, password changes, and other security events.

Each user in your organization controls their own preferences independently.

<Tip>
  Set up Slack or Discord for team-wide visibility, and use email for on-call engineers who need direct alerts.
</Tip>
