<Info>
  Teams are available on **Nixopus Cloud** Team and Enterprise plans.
</Info>

Teams let multiple people manage projects, deployments, and settings under a single organization.

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full access. Manage billing, invite/remove members, delete the organization. |
| **Admin** | Manage projects, deployments, domains, and team members. Cannot manage billing or delete the organization. |
| **Member** | View and deploy projects. Cannot change settings or manage team members. |

## Invite members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Teams">
    Click **Teams** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send an invite">
    Enter the email address and select a role. Click **Invite**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the invite">
    The invited user receives an email with a link to join your organization. They need a Nixopus account to accept.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage members

From the **Teams** page, you can change roles or remove members. Pending invitations are listed separately and can be revoked before they're accepted.

## Team plan benefits

The Team plan includes up to 10 members, 2,000 monthly AI credits shared across the team, and priority support. Need more seats? Contact us about the Enterprise plan.
